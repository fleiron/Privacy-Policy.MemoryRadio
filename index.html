Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2025

Welcome to MemoryRadio (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number). The data you optionally enter (such as preferences or usage data) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

We may collect anonymized technical data for analytics and crash reporting, such as:

  • App version and device model
  • Usage data (e.g., which features are used)
  • Purchase events (via RevenueCat)

2. Purchases and Payments

The App offers premium content via a one-time purchase priced at $4.99 USD (or equivalent in other countries, e.g., $5.99). This purchase grants you lifetime access to all premium features.

Purchases are securely processed by Apple. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Purchase status is managed via RevenueCat, a third-party service that uses anonymous User IDs and may use device identifiers for fraud prevention and purchase validation purposes.

3. Data Security

We follow industry-standard practices to ensure data integrity and security. Since the app stores data locally and does not collect personal data, privacy risks are minimized.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Anonymized purchase-related events may be shared with RevenueCat strictly to operate and support in-app purchases.

5. Your Rights

You can manage or restore your purchase at any time through your Apple ID account settings.
